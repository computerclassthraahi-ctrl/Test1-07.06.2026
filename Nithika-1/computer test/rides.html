Wonderla features some of India's most thrilling roller coasters, like the famous Recoil, a reverse-looping coaster that shoots you through twists at 80 kmph before repeating it backward.


A giant wheel—commonly known as a Ferris wheel or observation wheel—is a towering amusement ride consisting of a massive rotating upright wheel with multiple passenger-carrying gondolas attached to its rim.


Water slides are recreational chutes that use continuously flowing water to reduce friction, allowing riders to glide safely into a pool
